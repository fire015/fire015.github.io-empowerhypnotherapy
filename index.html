How I can help you

Hi, my name is Emma Margolin and I run EM-Power Hypnotherapy, a clinical Solution Focused Hypnotherapy practice. I cover the following areas: Radlett, Elstree & Borehamwood, Shenley, Mill Hill, Hendon, Golders Green, Hampstead and other areas online.

Solution Focused Hypnotherapy is a type of talking therapy that combines the use of psychotherapy with hypnosis. As a modern psychotherapeutic approach I use practical, structured and well-researched strategies to help people make significant, positive changes in their lives.

I can help with and specialise in the following:

  • Low Moods
  • Business Performance
